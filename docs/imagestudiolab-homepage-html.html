FAQ

The questions we hear most.

Do I need Midjourney?

No. Every prompt is built for ChatGPT (with GPT Image / DALL·E) and Gemini Nano Banana Pro. Both work. Many buyers also use Adobe Firefly Generative Edit for relighting prompts. We deliberately do not require Midjourney — that's the whole point.

Will the AI render the prompt perfectly every time?

Be honest: no AI tool produces perfect output 100% of the time. Realistic expectation: 4–6 generations per prompt, then cherry-pick the best. That's how every successful AI creator works.

Our packs ship with a troubleshooting page that names the 10 most common AI failures and exact phrases to fix them. We've baked that hard-won knowledge in.

What's the difference between the single packs and the Studio Vault?

Single packs ($14–29) get you one specific pack. The Studio Vault ($99 founder's price) gets you every pack we currently sell AND every pack we'll ever release in the future. We add new packs every 2–4 weeks.

If you'll buy more than 3 packs over the next year, the Vault is cheaper. Plus it includes commercial license — use generated outputs for client work or product photos.

How does "lifetime access" actually work on Gumroad?

The Studio Vault is one Gumroad product. When we ship a new pack, we update the Vault's file bundle. Gumroad automatically emails every past Vault buyer: "New file added — re-download to access." You click, download, get the new pack. Repeat for every future pack we ship.

It's the same mechanism Tatiana Tsiguleva and dozens of other Gumroad creators use for lifetime deals. It just works.

Single-pack buyers: personal use only. Studio Vault buyers: commercial license included. Use generated outputs for client work, product photos, ads, social media for your business — anywhere except reselling the prompts themselves.
